About Velocity

Our mission is to empower athletes at every level

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Olympic athletes, Velocity has been at the forefront of sports innovation for over a decade. We combine cutting-edge technology with athlete insights to create gear that performs when it matters most.

What began as a small startup focused on running shoes has grown into a comprehensive sports brand serving athletes across multiple disciplines. Our products are now used by professional teams and individual competitors worldwide.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great performance comes from the perfect combination of technology and human potential. Our gear is designed to enhance natural ability without compromising comfort or durability.

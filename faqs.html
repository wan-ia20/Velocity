Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Product Information

What materials are used in Velocity sports accessories?

Our sports accessories are made from high-quality, durable materials designed for athletic performance. We use moisture-wicking fabrics, lightweight polymers, and premium metals in our products. All materials are tested for durability, comfort, and performance under athletic conditions.

How do I choose the right size for sports gear?

We provide detailed size charts for each product category on our website. For clothing, measure your chest, waist, and hips and compare with our size chart. For equipment like gloves or shoes, we recommend measuring your hand or foot and referring to our specific sizing guides. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for comfort during physical activity.

Are Velocity products suitable for professional athletes?

Yes! Many professional athletes use Velocity products. Our gear is designed to meet the demands of high-performance athletics. We work with sports professionals during our product development process to ensure our accessories can withstand intense training and competition conditions.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Next-Day Delivery: Available for most locations (order before 2 PM local time)

International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can view the shipping cost during checkout by entering your destination country. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes that may apply.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our customer service team. After this window, your order moves to our fulfillment center and cannot be modified. If you need to make changes after this period, you may need to return the items once received (following our return policy).

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused items in their original packaging with tags attached. For defective products, we provide a full refund or exchange within 90 days of purchase. To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Portal or contact our customer service team.

Some items like personalized products or clearance items may be final sale and not eligible for return unless defective.

Do your products come with a warranty?

Yes, most Velocity products come with a manufacturer's warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. The warranty period varies by product:

  • Sports equipment: 1-2 years depending on the product
  • Electronics: 1 year
  • Apparel: 6 months for manufacturing defects

Please refer to the specific product page or documentation for warranty details.

How do I make a warranty claim?

To make a warranty claim, please contact our customer service team with your order number and details about the issue. We may require photos or a description of the defect. For eligible claims, we will typically repair or replace the item, or provide a refund at our discretion.

Please note that the warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, accidents, modifications, or normal wear and tear.

Product Care

How should I clean and maintain my Velocity gear?

Proper care will extend the life of your sports accessories:

  • Apparel: Machine wash cold with like colors, tumble dry low
  • Equipment: Wipe with a damp cloth and mild soap, air dry completely
  • Electronics: Follow the specific cleaning instructions in the product manual
  • Footwear: Clean with a soft brush and mild detergent, air dry away from direct heat

Always check the care label on each product for specific instructions.

How do I store sports equipment during off-season?

Proper storage is essential for maintaining your equipment:

  1. Clean all items thoroughly before storage
  2. Ensure everything is completely dry to prevent mold/mildew
  3. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  4. Use breathable storage containers rather than plastic bags
  5. For inflatable items, deflate partially to prevent stress on seams
  6. Loosen tension on items like racquet strings or bow limbs
